About PLAYARENA

PLAYARENA is a demo online store for building-block toys (LEGO®-style), where you can browse the collection, add products to your cart, and manage your account. The site features a modern interface optimized for both desktop and mobile.

Shopping & Products

  • Visit the Shop to browse all products loaded from the server (API).
  • You can sort by price and search by name directly on the store page.
  • Images and details (age range, piece count, theme) are displayed from the system data.

Account & Sign In

  • Use Sign In to create a new account or log in with your email and password.
  • After signing in, the account icon changes color and you can access your profile page while your session is active.
  • Sessions use a security token (JWT); sign out if you are on a shared device.

Cart

  • Products added from the Shop page are stored in your browser (local cart).
  • Open the Cart to view details, update quantities, remove items, and see the total (including estimated tax and shipping).
  • The badge on the cart icon updates to reflect the total number of items.

Technical & Support

If the Shop page fails to load products, make sure the backend server is running (default port is localhost:3000) and try refreshing the page.
